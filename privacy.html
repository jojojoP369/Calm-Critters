Privacy Policy

Calm Critters — Meditation for Kids

🌟 Our Promise

Calm Critters is built with your child's safety in mind. We believe young users deserve the strongest privacy protections. This policy explains exactly what data we do (and don't) collect.

📱 Data That Stays On Your Device

All app data is stored locally on your device only using your browser's localStorage. This includes:

  • Age group selection
  • Stars and streak progress
  • Feelings check-in history
  • Curriculum progress
  • Session results and mood check-in scores

This data never leaves your device. We cannot see it, access it, or share it with anyone. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser data.

🔊 Text-to-Speech Service

Calm Critters uses a text-to-speech service (ElevenLabs) to generate the gentle voice that guides your child through exercises. When a meditation or exercise plays:

  • The script text (the words Bear says) is sent to our server, which forwards it to ElevenLabs to generate audio.
  • No personal information about your child is ever sent — only the pre-written exercise scripts.
  • Audio responses are cached on your device so the same phrase doesn't need to be re-generated.

🎵 Ambient Sounds

Background nature sounds (ocean waves, birds, rain) are loaded from an external source (Moodist). No personal data is sent when loading these sounds.

🎤 Microphone Access

The Breath Coach games can optionally use your device's microphone to detect breathing. This is:

  • Completely optional — your child can tap the screen instead
  • Processed locally only — audio from the microphone is analyzed on your device in real-time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted
  • Permission-based — your browser will ask for permission before the microphone is used

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

Calm Critters does not use cookies, advertising trackers, analytics services, or any third-party tracking tools. We have no way to identify individual users.

👨‍👩‍👧 Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Calm Critters is designed for children ages 3 and older. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not collect personal information from children
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not display advertising
  • We do not share any data with third parties for commercial purposes

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

📧 Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or Calm Critters, please reach out to us at our support channels.
